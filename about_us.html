About Us

Welcome to TravelTips! We know that the best travel experiences come from the recommendations of people who share your outlook and are looking for the same kind of experiences. Here at TravelTips, we are a small but well-traveled team who want to pass on our knowledge and experiences to make sure that your holidays are as perfect as can be and you come back refreshed and full of the wonder of the world!

Meet Our Team

Dave Train

Dave founded TravelTips in 1989 after an illuminating night out in Bruges. Ever since then he has sought out new experiences and is passionate about passing this knowledge on to others. Dave is responsible for selecting and curating the experiences you will find on this site.
